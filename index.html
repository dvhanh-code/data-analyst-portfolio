Sales Performance Dashboard
Retail sales analysis project focused on identifying revenue drivers, regional profit patterns, product performance, and the impact of discounting on profitability.

Business Goal

  • Understand which categories and regions drive revenue and profit
  • Identify patterns in product-level performance
  • Assess how discounting affects profitability

Key Findings

  • Regional profitability differs significantly
  • Higher discount levels can reduce profit margins

Customer Churn Analysis
Telecom churn analysis project focused on customer retention patterns, high-risk segments, and the effects of contract type, tenure, and monthly charges on churn.

Business Goal

  • Identify customer characteristics linked to churn
  • Detect high-risk retention segments
  • Generate business recommendations to reduce churn

Key Findings

  • Month-to-month contracts show higher churn risk
  • Shorter tenure is strongly associated with churn
  • Customers with higher charges show greater churn probability
